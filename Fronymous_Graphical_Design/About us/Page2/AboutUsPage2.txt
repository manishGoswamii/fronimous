This project aims to provide space for such patients where they can openly express their 
condition to the doctors without revealing anything about their identity.


How it works?

Fronymous provide user an anonymous access to different doctors and psychiatrists, 
where they can actually express their condition and get the honest review and suggestion
for them.

Without feeling judged or weird user can ask anything about their feelings, thoughts,
sleep, dreams, etc.

And the other object is to make people understand that they are not alone, people 
across globe face the same thing. This is achieved by provided different articles, news, 
and references on website.
